Research:

Physical Wellbeing:

  1. Exercise and Physical Activity: Engaging in regular exercise and physical activity is essential for maintaining physical health. It includes activities such as walking, jogging, swimming, and strength training.
  2. Nutrition and Diet: Consuming a balanced diet rich in fruits, vegetables, whole grains, lean proteins, and healthy fats is crucial for providing the body with essential nutrients and promoting overall health.
  3. Sleep Hygiene: Getting adequate and quality sleep is vital for physical wellbeing. Practicing good sleep hygiene, such as maintaining a consistent sleep schedule and creating a conducive sleep environment, is important for restorative sleep.

Mental Wellbeing

  1. Stress Management: Developing effective strategies for managing stress, such as mindfulness, relaxation techniques, and time management, is crucial for maintaining mental wellbeing.
  2. Self-care Practices: Engaging in self-care activities that promote relaxation, enjoyment, and personal fulfillment, such as hobbies, leisure activities, and self-reflection, is important for mental health maintenance.

The areas I wish to focus on in this project are stress management and heartrate.
These are 2 very important areas for teenagers who may struggle with stress, anxiety and depression.
To get a better understanding of my target audience I sent a survey throughout the school.
